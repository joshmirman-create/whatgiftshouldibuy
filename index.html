How does this work?

Answer five quick questions — who the gift is for, their age, one thing you know about them, your budget, and the occasion. We recommend the single most specific, thoughtful gift that fits what you told us, plus a book and alternatives.

What if I barely know the person?

One real detail is enough. "He loves his boat" or "she's always cold" gives us plenty to work with. We're built for the hard cases, not just the easy ones.
