Questions people are asking right now

What is a good Mother's Day gift that is not flowers?

Something she would want but would not buy for herself. A really good body oil. A book from an author she has been meaning to try. A cooking class she mentioned once. The specific version of any of these lands harder than flowers every time.

What do you get a mom who says she does not want anything?

She wants time and to feel seen. An experience you arrange for her, or a handwritten letter with three specific things you noticed her do this year. Both of these cost less than most gifts and land harder than almost anything you could buy.

What is a good Mother's Day gift from the kids?

Anything with their handwriting or voice on it. A handmade card, a recorded video of them saying what they love about her, or a coupon book they made themselves. Supplement it with something practical she will use, but the kid-made thing is the gift she keeps.

How much should I spend on a Mother's Day gift?

Enough that it feels considered. For most situations, somewhere between $40 and $100 is right. The specificity matters more than the number. A $30 book she has been wanting beats a $100 generic spa set every time.
