Questions people actually ask

What are the most popular gifts for 5 year olds?

Magnetic tiles, science kits, art supplies, bikes, and anything tied to what they are currently obsessed with.

What educational toys are good for 5 year olds?

Science kits, magnetic tiles, and early reading tools all build real skills while feeling like play.

How much should I spend on a gift for a 5 year old?

$30 to $75 is a typical range. Magnetic tiles are expensive but last years. Science kits are great at $25 to $40.
