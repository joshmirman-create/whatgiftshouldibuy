Questions people actually ask

What do you get a boyfriend who says he does not want anything?

He wants something. He just does not want to ask for it. Go practical and quality: something he uses constantly but at a noticeably better level than he currently has. A good kitchen knife, better headphones, or a game he has been waiting to play.

How much should I spend on a boyfriend gift?

Enough that it feels considered, not so much that it feels like pressure. For most relationships, somewhere between $50 and $150 is the right range. The specificity matters more than the number.

What are good last-minute gifts for a boyfriend?

A gift card to a restaurant you both love, a streaming subscription to something he has been curious about, or a really good bottle of whatever he drinks. Fast to get, easy to give, and still thoughtful if you pick the right version.
