Frequently asked questions

How do I find a gift for a teen I barely know?

Tell us one thing — "obsessed with Minecraft," "really into drawing anime," "always has headphones in." That's enough to get a specific, non-embarrassing result.

What's a good gift for a teen that isn't just cash?

Something connected to what they're into right now. A book or graphic novel in their favorite series, quality gear for their hobby, or something they'd love but wouldn't buy themselves.
