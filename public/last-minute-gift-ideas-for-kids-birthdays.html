Questions people actually ask

What is a good last-minute birthday gift for a kid?

An Amazon same-day delivery item tied to their interest, a gift card with a plan attached, or a local bookstore pickup. All three look considered.

What can I buy for a child's birthday at the last minute?

Art supplies, card games, and books are all available at major retailers and take zero planning to give well.

How do I make a last-minute gift look thoughtful?

Add a handwritten note explaining what you plan to do with the gift card, or pick something specifically tied to their current interest rather than something generic.
