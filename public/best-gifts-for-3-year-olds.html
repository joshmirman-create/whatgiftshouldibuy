Questions people actually ask

What are the best toys for a 3 year old?

Pretend play toys, building sets, art supplies, and anything tied to their current obsession. The gift that connects to what they love right now always wins.

What should I get a 3 year old for their birthday?

Something that feeds their current obsession. Ask the parent what the child talks about constantly right now and go from there.

How much should I spend on a gift for a 3 year old?

Between $20 and $50 is the right range for most situations. Quality matters more than quantity at this age.
