Questions people actually ask

What do preschool teachers actually want as gifts?

Gift cards to classroom supply stores, coffee shop gift cards with genuine notes, and quality self-care items all land better than decorative objects.

How much should I spend on a preschool teacher gift?

$20 to $40 is standard. A well-chosen $25 gift card beats a $50 item she does not need.

What should a child give their teacher at the end of the year?

A handwritten or dictated note from the child plus a practical gift card is the most appreciated combination.
